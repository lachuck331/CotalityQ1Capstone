Methodology

We start with standard baselines, then move to imbalance-aware tree boosting after observing that rare fire events are overwhelmed by the negative class.

1

Baselines First

Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Linear SVM establish initial performance and failure modes.

2

Extreme Imbalance

Positive fire labels are very rare, so high overall accuracy can still hide weak fire-event detection.

3

XGBoost + Optuna

GPU-accelerated XGBoost with hyperparameter tuning targets stronger ranking and minority-class behavior.

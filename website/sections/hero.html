Predicting Wildfire Burn
Probability across California

We fuse climate, vegetation, land cover, terrain, and historical fire perimeters into a statewide monthly dataset (2000–2024), then train rare-event classifiers to estimate burn probability over an 800 m grid. Model-ready records used in modeling span March 2000 to December 2024.

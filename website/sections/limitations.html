Limitations & Next Steps

Relative to the best baseline, tuned XGBoost improves temporal validation PR-AUC but remains very low (0.0113), while random-split performance is much higher. This gap indicates sensitivity to nonstationarity and severe class imbalance.

For stakeholders, the current system should be interpreted as a research-stage risk signal, not a finalized operational trigger. The next phase focuses on improving out-of-time reliability and reducing false confidence in minority-event predictions.

Pipeline

The whole point: your audience understands the end-to-end system in 15 seconds.

1) Ingest
Download + standardize raw sources
Climate (PRISM), vegetation (NDVI), land cover (NLCD), terrain (DEM), fire perimeters (MTBS).
2) Feature engineering
Join to a monthly grid
Consistent spatial indexing, monthly aggregation, and clean training tables.
3) Train
GPU XGBoost baseline
Imbalance-aware training strategy and hyperparameter tuning.
4) Evaluate
Ranking + stability checks
Metrics + map-based validation to catch nonsense predictions.
5) Deploy-ready
Artifacts for a product
Heatmaps, reports, and a path to tile serving / API integration.

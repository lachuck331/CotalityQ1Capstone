Pipeline

The pipeline brings together PRISM climate, MODIS vegetation, NLCD land cover, USGS terrain, and MTBS wildfire perimeters on a shared monthly California grid. Each source is aligned to the same spatial footprint before being merged into one statewide analysis table.

From there, the workflow filters incomplete coverage, adds derived predictors, and separates a temporal holdout before model training. Click nodes in the graph for dataset provenance and high-level stage descriptions.

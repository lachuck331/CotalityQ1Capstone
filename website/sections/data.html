Data sources

Public datasets were harmonized to a common statewide ~800m monthly grid for 2000–2024 wildfire burn-probability modeling.

Climate
PRISM
Monthly climate predictors (ppt, tdmean, tmax, vpdmax) for statewide California.
Vegetation
MODIS NDVI
Monthly NDVI vegetation signal (Feb 2000 onward; Jan 2000 missing by design).
Land cover
NLCD
Annual landcover classes mapped to model categories and one-hot encoded features.
Terrain
USGS DEM
Static terrain predictors (elevation, slope, aspect) aligned to the analysis grid.
Fire history
MTBS
Fire perimeters rasterized into monthly burned/non-burned labels.
Engineering
Unified Grid
Model-ready feature table with lag/log transforms, one-hot encoding, and train/test/temporal-validation splits.
